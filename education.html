MY EDUCATIONAL JOURNEY

Building the Foundation for a Brighter Future




Early Beginnings

Tadika Al-Irfan is where my education started. It is a place full of fond memories and beginnings of learning. It was here that I began to identify the letters of the alphabet and the basic numbers, which are the basis of what I know now. This was an important first step in building the foundation of my learning, and it sparked an interest that continues to grow to this day.

Primary School

After that, I entered the realm of primary school at SK Sultan Mudzaffar Shah. Here, the mastery of fields such as Bahasa Melayu, Mathematics, Science, and History helped me understand the basics of my studies. Furthermore, extracurricular activities, such as sports, associations, and competitions, help me improve my talents and increase my self-confidence.

Secondary School

I then studied at Maktab Mahmud Merbok. This school not only prioritizes the academic performance of its students, but it also incorporates Islamic value in all aspects of education and daily life. In addition, I was educated to be more disciplined and always have a good relationship with Allah and fellow human beings because the school environment is full of Islamic practices, such as congregational prayer, recitation of the Quran and weekly tazkirah.

Current Persuits

My educational journey now continues at Universiti Teknologi MARA (UiTM). This institution of higher learning allowed me to pursue my ambitions and build a solid foundation for a better future. Also, joining clubs and societies gives me the opportunity to share ideas, work in teams, and build a social network.
