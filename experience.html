SWEET EXPERIENCE

Like adding a new page in the book of life, new experiences teach us many things and make us wiser and ready to face challenges. Experience gives us a richer perspective and value, teaching us things that are not taught in books.




Intern Life

Industrial training at MPSPK is a very valuable experience. Not only did I gain a deeper understanding of aspects of municipal administration, but I also gained an understanding of how each task carried out has a significant impact on the community. I got new inspiration and drive to grow here.

PMDS

I appreciate the incredible experiences I had as a Student Destination Week (PMDS) Mentor twice, in 2023 and 2024. It was not just an opportunity to lead, but also an opportunity to inspire and guide my peers while improving myself as a a more responsible and wise person.

Volunteer SP Berselawat

Volunteering for the SP Berselawat program is a heart-warming experience. It is not only an opportunity to make a donation, but also a way to enjoy visiting with a cheerful and united community. This program gave me knowledge about the meaning of giving and sharing.

Active Club at UiTM

I feel that being a volunteer organized by the Faith Club is a very valuable experience. Giving time and energy to events like this, not only brings you closer to the public but also enriches the soul with shared knowledge and inspiration. Every moment feels precious when we can learn and help at the same time.
